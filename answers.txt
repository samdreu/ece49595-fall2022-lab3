a) In a non-agile project, the team leader is equivalent to the Product owner.
Their role in the team is to set direction for the ream, handle release management,
and stakeholder management. They are needed for all of those responsibilities and
are needed for them in the team.

b) The scrum master is responsible for all the scrum related tasks like JIRA tooling,
facilitating sprints and meetings, and all standups are led by the scrum master.

c)
Sprint planning:
    What to complete in coming sprints
    Determine story points and assign stories to team
Daily Scrum:
    Discuss work completed, work plan for the day, any blockers
Sprint Retrospective:
    Discuss work complete
    Move incomplete work into backlog

d) 
Epic: Large body of work that can be broken down into a number of smaller stories
Story: Software system requirement expressed in a few short sentences
Bug: A problem that impairs or prevents the functions of the project
Task: A particular piece of work to be done
